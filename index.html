Overview

ThisWorkshop is intended to have talks by theoreticians and experimentalists primarily from France, Germany and India. This will be followed by intense discussions on the following topics :


  • Multifragmentation

  • Collective flow

  • Fusion-Fission

  • Sub-threshold Particle Production

  • Hadrons in matter and hyper nuclei

    • TheAim of workshop is to explore possibilities of future joint collaborations between scientists from east and west involving both theoreticians and experimentalists of the field. We also intend to have training sessions of various computer programs used in the field of intermediate energy heavy ion reactions.
